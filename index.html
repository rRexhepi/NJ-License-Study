The test in one paragraph

The NJ MVC knowledge test is 50 multiple-choice questions. You need 40 correct (80%) to pass. Topics: traffic laws, road signs, safe driving, alcohol/drugs, license rules, and what to do in emergencies. Use the chapters below as your map, the flashcards to drill terms, and the quiz to check yourself. When you can score 90%+ on the mock test consistently, you're ready.
